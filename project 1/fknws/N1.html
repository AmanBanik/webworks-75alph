Zomato Clone "FeastFast" Secures $50 Million in Oversubscribed Series C Funding Round

May 26, 2025

Bengaluru, India â€“ FeastFast, the burgeoning food delivery platform that has garnered a reputation as a formidable "Zomato clone" in the Indian market, proudly announced today the successful closure of its Series C funding round, raising a substantial $50 million. The oversubscribed round was spearheaded by the globally recognized venture capital giant, Apex Ventures, with robust participation from its existing investor base, signaling strong confidence in FeastFast's disruptive business model and rapid growth trajectory.

Since its inception three years ago, FeastFast has strategically focused on penetrating the untapped potential of India's tier-2 and tier-3 cities. This calculated approach, combined with a hyper-local operational strategy and a meticulously built network of independent delivery riders, has enabled the company to achieve explosive growth figures that have surprised many industry veterans. The platform's intuitive user interface and diverse culinary offerings have resonated deeply with consumers outside the traditional metropolitan hubs.

"This fresh infusion of capital will be absolutely instrumental in turbocharging our expansion efforts," stated an enthusiastic Priya Sharma, CEO of FeastFast, during a press conference held this morning. "We are committed to significantly scaling our operational footprint, making substantial enhancements to our proprietary technology stack, and aggressively broadening our market reach into even more underserved regions of the country. Our core mission remains unwavering: to democratize food delivery, making it both accessible and economically viable for every single household, regardless of their location."

Industry analysts have been closely monitoring FeastFast's remarkable ascent. Many now widely believe that the company's aggressive market penetration tactics, coupled with its relentless pursuit of operational efficiencies and a deep understanding of regional culinary preferences, have strategically positioned it as a truly formidable contender in India's highly competitive food technology landscape. The platform's ability to onboard local restaurants and cater to specific regional tastes has been a key differentiator.

Looking ahead, FeastFast has outlined ambitious plans for the utilization of the newly acquired funds. A significant portion will be allocated towards the further development and refinement of its cutting-edge, AI-driven logistics algorithms, which aim to optimize delivery routes and reduce dispatch times even further. The company also intends to forge new and strategic partnerships with a wider array of local and national restaurant chains, thereby enriching its menu offerings and providing more choices to its burgeoning customer base. Furthermore, a substantial investment will be made in recruiting top-tier talent across all departments, from engineering and product development to sales and customer support, ensuring the continued innovation and smooth operation of the platform. The Series C round marks a pivotal moment in FeastFast's journey, setting the stage for its next phase of exponential growth.
